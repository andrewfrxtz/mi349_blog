The Building of this Blog

Day One

Andrew Fritz
June 6, 2023 The first day was full of trying to remember how to structure HTML. I watched the review videos to jog my memory, and then went right to work. I thought that starting with the About and Contact pages of the site would be the easiest to tackle, because I would have less to write than the homepage (this one). I did the about page first, since it was very similar to the work we did in the Hello World: Part 1 that we did last week. Adding paragraphs and images were a great start to my initial build of the site. I made great progress, and only had minor changes after that. I did have some validation errors to fix, but it was nothing too complex. I was proud of myself for remembering as much as I did from the week before!

About Me Page:

After that, I had to tackle the more challenging aspect of this blog - a form. It was a new process that we learned in the lecture materials this week, so I thought it would be best to just push myself through and get it done. I struggled with the syntax a little bit, but the documentation I had available helped me to format it correctly and get all of the options I wanted. After a few more pushes, and a lot of syntax changes and validations, I was able get it where I wanted. It did take me a bit longer than I was expecting, which did discourage me a little bit.

Contact Me Page:

After getting these pages close to where I wanted them, I decided to take a break. I wanted to make sure I could keep my attention to detail strong while doing the homepage! I decided to stop here since I believe that I had a great amount of progress made.

Day Two

Andrew Fritz
June 7, 2023 This is the day where I tackled the homepage and the final touches on the pages from the previous day. I needed to review the homepage's requirements, and then build them all out. While I was reviewing the pages and some of the example code from the lecture videos, I realized I had missed something. I wanted to make a second header for additional information about each page, so I added that to each of my pages. You can see the differences below.

About Me Page:

Updates to the About page. As you can see, the change is very small, but I changed the second header of the about page to make it different from the second header on the index page.

Contact Me Page:

Updates to the Contact page. These edits helped me to make the pages look a little better and break up the navigation pane.

Next, I had to build a table that would let me display some data. I made up some data, breaking down what I did for each section on each day, and a status update on each one!
